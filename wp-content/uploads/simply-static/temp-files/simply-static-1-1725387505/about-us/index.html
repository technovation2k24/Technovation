OVERVIEW:

Decrypt cryptic codes to reveal elusive secrets, merge images to expose hidden messages, and forge connections to piece together intricate puzzles. Prepare yourself for an exhilarating adventure where fun and challenge converge in unexpected ways!

RULES:

  • Per team only 2 persons can participate
  • Not necessary both are in same college
  • For calculation mobile phones not allowed

ABOUT:

Crack decrypted codes to unlock secrets!

  • If both 2 team got same point the two teams are consider as a winners

Time: 1 minute (Per question)
